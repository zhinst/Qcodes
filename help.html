Get Help¶

Welcome to the QCoDeS community! We would love to have you join us and share your ideas.

Please help us spread the word and grow the community!

Chat¶

We discuss things on slack, join us! You can find the maintainers’ email on their commits. Please send a mail to Jens.Nielsen(at)microsoft.com if you want to join the slack channel. Please include a few lines about who you are and how you use or want to use QCoDeS.

More¶

For more info on how to contribute check out the Community Guide section.
